Triple-A in Action

How we helped a regional retailer unlock new market opportunities

The Challenge

A fast-growing retailer with 200+ stores was struggling to expand into new markets. Their supply chain data was fragmented across multiple legacy systems, making it impossible to identify demand patterns, optimize inventory placement, or respond to regional preferences. Expansion decisions were based on intuition rather than data.

Our Triple-A Solution

Agility:

Built real-time dashboards showing store-level demand, inventory turns, and regional trends

Adaptability:

Integrated third-party demographic and competitor data to model new market opportunities

Alignment:

Created shared KPIs across merchandising, operations, and finance teams

The Growth Outcome

Within 12 months of implementing the new data platform:

  • ▸ 15% revenue increase in new market regions
  • ▸ 22% reduction in stockouts across all stores
  • ▸ 30% faster decision-making on expansion locations

Research shows that companies with aligned supply chains report 2-3x higher revenue growth and market share gains compared to competitors. Our approach is grounded in this evidence.
